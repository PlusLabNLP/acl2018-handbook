Abstract#==#FrameNet is a lexico-semantic dataset that embodies the theory of frame
semantics. Like other semantic databases, FrameNet is incomplete. We augment it
via the paraphrase database, PPDB, and gain a three-fold increase in coverage
at 65% precision.
