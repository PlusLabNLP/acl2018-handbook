Abstract#==#This paper presents a metaphor interpretation pipeline based on abductive
inference. In this framework following (Hobbs, 1992) metaphor interpretation
is modelled as a part of the general discourse processing problem, such that
the overall discourse coherence is supported. We present an experimental
evaluation of the proposed approach using linguistic data in English and
Russian.
