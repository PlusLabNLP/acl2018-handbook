Abstract#==#We present WiBi, an approach to the automatic creation of a bitaxonomy for
Wikipedia, that is, an integrated taxonomy of Wikipage pages and categories. We
leverage the information available in either one of the taxonomies to reinforce
the creation of the other taxonomy. Our experiments show higher quality and
coverage than state-of-the-art resources like DBpedia, YAGO, MENTA, WikiNet and
WikiTaxonomy. WiBi is available at http://wibitaxonomy.org.
