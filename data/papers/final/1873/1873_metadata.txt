Abstract#==#This paper studies strategies to model word formation in NMT using rich linguistic 
information, namely a word segmentation approach that goes beyond splitting into 
substrings by considering fusional morphology. Our linguistically sound segmentation 
is combined with a method for target-side inflection to accommodate modeling word 
formation. The best system variants employ source-side morphological analysis and 
model complex target-side words, improving over a standard system.
