Abstract#==#This paper describes RACAI’s (Research Institute for Artificial Intelligence)
hybrid grammatical error correction system. This system was validated during
the participation into the CONLL Shared Task on Grammatical Error Correction.
We
offer an analysis of the types of errors detected and corrected by our system
and we present the necessary steps to reproduce our experiment
