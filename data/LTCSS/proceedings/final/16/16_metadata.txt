Abstract#==#This paper documents an ongoing effort to assess whether party group
affiliation of participants in European Parliament debates can be
automatically predicted on the basis of the content of their speeches, using
a support vector machine multi-class model. The work represents a joint
effort between researchers within Political Science and Language Technology.
