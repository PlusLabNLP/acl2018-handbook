Abstract#==#This paper describes the system jointly developed by members of the Departament
de Llenguatges i Sistemes Informàtics at Universitat d'Alacant and the
Prompsit Language Engineering company for the shared translation task of the
2014 Workshop on Statistical Machine Translation. We present a phrase-based
statistical machine translation system whose phrase table is enriched with
information obtained from dictionaries and shallow-transfer rules like those
used in rule-based machine translation. The novelty of our approach lies in the
fact that the transfer rules used were not written by humans, but automatically
inferred from a parallel corpus.
