Abstract#==#We describe the CMU systems submitted to the 2014 WMT shared task in machine
translation. We participated in two language pairs, German-English and
Hindi-English.
Our particular innovations include: a label coarsening scheme for syntactic
tree-to-tree translation, a host of new discriminative features, several
modules to create "synthetic translation options" that can generalize beyond
what is directly observed in the parallel training data, and a method of
combining the output of multiple word aligners to uncover extra phrase pairs
and grammar rules.
