Abstract#==#This paper describes the IPN-UPV participation on the English-to-Hindi
translation task from WMT 2014 International Evaluation Campaign. The system
presented is based on Moses and enhanced with deep learning by means of a
source-context feature function. This feature depends on the input sentence to
translate, which makes it more challenging to adapt it into the Moses
framework. This work reports the
experimental details of the system putting special emphasis on: how the feature
function is integrated in Moses and how the deep learning representations are
trained and used.
