Abstract#==#We present the CimS submissions to the
2014 Shared Task for the language pair
EN→DE. We address the major problems
that arise when translating into German:
complex nominal and verbal morphol-
ogy, productive compounding and flexible
word ordering by applying a morphology-
aware translation system that allows to
handle word formation issues.
