Abstract#==#We describe Stanford's participation in the French-English and English-German
tracks of the 2014 Workshop on Statistical Machine Translation (WMT).  Our
systems use large feature sets, word classes, and an optional unconstrained
language model.  Among constrained systems, ours performed the best according
to uncased BLEU: 36.0% for French-English and 20.9% for English-German.
