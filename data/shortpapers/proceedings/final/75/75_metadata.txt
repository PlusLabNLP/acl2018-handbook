Abstract#==#Internet users are keen on creating different kinds of morphs to avoid
censorship, express strong sentiment or humor. For example, in Chinese social
media, users often use the entity morph “方便面 (Instant Noodles)” to
refer to “周永康 (Zhou Yongkang)” because it shares one character “康
(Kang)” with the well-known brand of instant noodles “康师傅 (Master
Kang)”. We developed a wide variety of novel approaches to automatically
encode proper and interesting morphs, which can effectively pass decoding
tests.
