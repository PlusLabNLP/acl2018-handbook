Abstract#==#In this paper we study the use of sentence-level dialect identification in
optimizing machine translation system selection when translating mixed dialect
input. 
We test our approach on Arabic, a prototypical diglossic language; and we
optimize the combination of four different machine translation systems. 
Our best result improves over the best single MT system baseline by 1.0% BLEU
and over a strong system selection baseline by 0.6% BLEU on a blind test set.
