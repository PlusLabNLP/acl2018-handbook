Abstract#==#We describe the design and use of the Stanford CoreNLP toolkit, an extensible
pipeline that provides core natural language analysis. This toolkit is quite
widely used, both in the research NLP community and also among commercial and
government users of open source NLP technology. We suggest that this follows
from a simple, approachable design, straightforward interfaces, the inclusion
of robust and good quality analysis components, and not requiring use of a
large amount of associated baggage.
